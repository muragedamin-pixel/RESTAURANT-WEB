Our Story

The REAL Experience

Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, REAL RESTAURANT & HOTEL is where authentic Kenyan heritage meets world-class hospitality. We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere that makes every guest feel at home — whether you're dining in for the evening or staying for the week.
